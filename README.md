# Manizha Salomova - QA Portfolio

I am a Senior Quality Engineer focused on manual testing, SaaS workflows, billing systems, analytics, and release quality. My strength is understanding how a feature should behave, finding the risks before users do, and communicating issues clearly enough for Product and Engineering teams to make good decisions quickly.

I use automation and AI as accelerators in my QA process. AI helps me speed up test idea generation, edge-case brainstorming, documentation cleanup, and repetitive analysis, but I still own the testing strategy, product judgment, defect investigation, and final quality recommendations.

## How I Use AI In QA

I use AI as a QA assistant, not as a replacement for QA judgment. Examples:

- Brainstorming edge cases after I review requirements
- Expanding test coverage ideas for complex workflows
- Organizing notes into cleaner test cases or bug reports
- Creating test data variations faster
- Summarizing exploratory testing observations
- Comparing expected behavior against acceptance criteria to look for gaps

My responsibility remains deciding what matters, validating the product behavior, investigating issues, and communicating risk clearly to the team.

## Professional Positioning

I bring the most value in QA roles where the product has complex workflows, cross-team dependencies, customer impact, and a need for clear communication. I am comfortable testing manually, supporting automation, reviewing requirements, asking product questions, and helping teams release with confidence.
